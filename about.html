About Me

Hi! I'm a third year college student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute majoring in Interactive Media and Game Development. I specialize in game design, 2D graphic design, UI/UX, and concept art.

My go-to tools are the Adobe Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Anime), ProCreate, and Autodesk 3ds Max.

I'm passionate about creating games that are satisfying in design and aesthetics, as well as accessible for all audiences.

I'm capable of working in both English and Mandarin. Please feel free to reach out at my contact page!
